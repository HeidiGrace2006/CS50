Linear:
    * Starts at the beginning, goes through each one
    * O(n)

Binary:
    * Starts in middle, bigger or smaller?
    * O(log n)

Selection Sort:
    * Go through entire list, take note of lowest... swap the 2 numbers. repeat until sorted.
    * O(n^2)

Bubble Sort:
    * Go through list.. look at 2 num at a time. Swap those 2 num to be right.
    * O(n^2)

Merge Sort:
    * Sort left half, sort right half, merge into new array from left of each section.
    * O(n log n)
