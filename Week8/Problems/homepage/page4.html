What are my intrests?

For starters, coding!
My first encounter with coding (apart from Scratch in elementary school) was in a highschool computers class my freshmen year.
I loved it. Prior to this I had started to become bored with school. Public school classes were too easy, and most of the work was busy work.
Coding, however, made me think differently. I was forced to problem solve. To learn something entirely new.
It was the first time I felt like a passion was a realistic career for me.

But one of my favorite intrests has always been piano.
I've taken piano lessons for 11 years, and have never lost intrest in the slightest.
One of the reasons I fell in love with learning coding was because it reminded me of learning piano:
something fun, challenging, an unlike the other skills in my day-to-day life.
Now that I've graduated, I'm moving on from piano lessons. But I hope I never move on from piano.
