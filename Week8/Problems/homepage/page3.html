What do I value?

The foundation of my values is my faith in God, the creator of this world, and specifically Jesus.
I know that a lot of people have heard of Jesus dying for them before, however, most don't know Him or His love for them.
We have all fallen short over and over again, and we can't make ammends on our own. Praise be to God who took our place and has saved us from death.
In all that I do, I try my best to do it for His glory. He died for me, so that I may live for Him.


I also value the importance of family, and I am so greatful for my own.
In addition to that, I am a proud American citizen who values freedom, work ethic, leadership, and humilty.
I wish that everyone knew that they were created with a purpose, and that they were loved before they were even born.
Which leads me to my final statement: I value the life that God has given me, and the time I have from Him to share His love with others.
